What is ML Colab?

ML Colab is a platform where you can connect with people, learn stuff about Machine Learning via the built-in algorithms, take notes and share them with your friends using teams.

All while being one click away from the latest news in the ML community.

What does it offer?

It's a space where you can connect with people that share the same passion as you, Machine Learning.

Add them, chat with them, create teams and share your notes with them.

All within an easy to use interface, with every option at the click of a button.
